Projects

Club Introduction Program

Honored to have served as a guest speaker at the Club Introduction Program hosted by the Career Circle Faculty Of Technology (FOTCC) at the Faculty Auditorium. It was a pleasure sharing insights and experiences with students and professionals eager to explore various clubs within the faculty. From discussing the importance of entrepreneurship to highlighting the role of technology in shaping the future, engaging with the audience was truly inspiring. Grateful for the opportunity to contribute to the academic and professional development of the attendees.

Train the Facilitator Workshop

Thrilled to have immersed myself in the Train the Facilitator Workshop hosted by the Centre For Monitoring Election Violence (CMEV) at the Royal Kandyan Hotel in Kandy from February 22nd to 24th, 2024. Over the three days, I delved deep into honing my facilitation skills, crucial for ensuring fair and peaceful elections. The interactive sessions and hands-on activities provided invaluable insights into effectively managing diverse groups and fostering constructive dialogue. Grateful for the opportunity to contribute towards promoting electoral integrity and grateful to CMEV for organizing such a transformative event.
